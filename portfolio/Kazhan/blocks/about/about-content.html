Natalia Halych

is an observer of life's collages.

Her unique vision of seemingly simple things and natural intuition make her work vivid and memorable.

Believing that the work of both the art director and the model should be thought out to the smallest detail and cause catharsis in the viewer.

Also at the age of 20, she conquered the Ukrainian underground scene with her musical group Brudni and worked in the Blevogue creative agancy. Upcoming works are a curious secret for her viewers...
